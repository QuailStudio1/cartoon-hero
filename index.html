Naruto Uzimaki

About Naruto

As a baby, his father sealed the nine tail fox known as Kurama inside of Naruto to save the village. Naruto grew up as an outcast because of this. He has a dream to be come the Hokage of the Hidden Leaf Village and surpass all those who look down upon him.

Naruto characteristics

  1. Naruto will never give up on things he believe in.
  2. He is extremely dedicated and motivated
  3. Simple minded and cheerful
